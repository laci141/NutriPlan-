Consensus live API · 5 sources
Physicians · Science Journalists · Fact-Checkers · Patients

Score any scientific claim from −1 to +1 (displayed 0–100). Aggregates evidence from all 5 sources, classifies stance (evidence-supports / inconclusive / evidence-refutes), and reports confidence and evidence strength. The fastest answer to "what does the science say about X?"

Reproducibility & Quality live API · metadata signals
